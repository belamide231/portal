Our Heritage Cebu Eastern College (CEC) is a private, non-sectarian Chinese-Filipino educational institution in Cebu City, founded in 1915. The school offers basic education from preschool to senior high, as well as college programs. Known for its blend of Philippine and Chinese cultural heritage, CEC provides academic instruction in English, Filipino, and Chinese, aiming to develop well-rounded students with strong cultural identity and community values.
